About this Project

Simple, practical carbon emission audit template tailored for the textile, chemical, and car-care product industries. This page explains objectives, method, and how to use the site.

Overview

The Carbon Emission Audit helps identify, quantify and analyse Carbon Emissions across textile and chemical industries. It covers:

  • Fuel combustion (boilers, furnaces)
  • Electricity usage for processes
  • Scope-based emission classification (Scope 1 & Scope 2)

Mission

To assist textile chemical domain industries in measuring their carbon footprint and suggesting cost-effective reduction opportunities — improving sustainability while maintaining productivity.

Objectives

  • Provide a clear emissions breakdown (Scope 1 / 2 )
  • Automate the conversion of raw activity data into accurate CO₂e reports using standard emission factors.
  • Offer actionable recommendations

Methodology

The project aims to establish a standardized carbon auditing framework for the textile, chemical, and car-care industries by automating activity-to-CO₂e conversion to provide visual insights and actionable mitigation strategies.

Global Impact

Our audit portal is designed to help industries meet international regulatory and sustainability standards:

  • ESG Transparency: Simplifies environmental reporting for stakeholders and investors.
  • Climate Action: Directly contributes to UN Sustainable Development Goal 13 (Climate Action).
  • Net-Zero 2070: Supports India's national commitment to total carbon neutrality.

How To Use

  • Go to Data to upload or view CSV records.
  • Open Carbon Audit to see results and suggested interventions.
  • Use this About page to understand purpose and process.
